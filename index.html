Welcome!

Throughout the course of this workshop, we will learn the fundamentals of programming. We will mostly focus on web development, however these skills are applicable to all types of programming (mobile applications, games, etc.). We will work together within a collaborative and supportive environment to learn to code. We will be working on projects that draw from from our experience to create solutions to issues in our commmunites.
